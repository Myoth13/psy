Key (future) features of the project.

Get free help

Project was created to provide help for the most vulnerable categories who do not have access to paid professionals. Using an AI model you will pass tests and get recommendations based on the results.

anonymity and confidentiality

Project is and will be opensource to provide the transparency. All results from chatbots will be anonimized and data will be used to improved the model. Your personal data will not be saved.

Making progress and share

You can save the results of your tests and keep track of your progress. You can also share your experience if you wish to help others

Group activities

Based on your results you can participate in group activities organized our volunteers. Psychologists are welcome to participate
